Keynote 1

Topic - Design Thinking
Speaker - Atheeth Shivalinga Prasad (PhD Scholar at CPDM,IISc)
Date - 28/08/2020
Timing - 10:30 AM - 11:30 AM

What are problems? Roadblocks or opportunities? It depends on the way we approach them. Design thinking is a systematic and iterative approach to problem solving that encourages solution based thinking. Today it is of great importance especially for product designing and in the startup ecospace where there is constant need for innovation.
