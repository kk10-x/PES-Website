IEEE PES Student Congress - 2020
Ideathon
In association with BMSCE Startup Cell MHRD IIC
Put on your thinking caps and throw in your most innovative ideas. Atlantis is a remote island isolated from the peninsular mainland of India. There are certain major problems that need to be addressed in Atlantis which reflect the problems faced in all major cities today. Solve one of them and give your opponents a run for their money in this ideathon, conducted in collaboration with BMSCE Startup Cell, IIC MHRD

Theme : Smart Cities
From August 28th to 30th,2020
Register ‎



Rules
  • The event is exclusively for students only.
  • The team has to be formed before day 1 wherein each team can have a maximum of 3 participants.
  • Team registration will be via google forms.
  • All team members have to collaborate and conceptualize to address one of the problem statements.
  • The filtering of the teams for the final round will be done on the basis of the quality and originality of the presentation submitted and usage of effective presentation skills to demonstrate the idea.
  • The teams must strictly follow the presentation format provided during the idea submission.
  • Top 10 teams will be shortlisted to proceed to the final round where they will have to present their ideas in front of the judges on day-3. Please note that the judges’ and jury members’ decision is final.

Submission Guidelines
  • Idea presentations have to be submitted on day 2 at 2:00 PM in Google slides format only.
  • The idea can be demonstrated using infographics/charts/graphs to show a more clear picture and to increase the chances of getting shortlisted for the final round.
  • The top 10 ideas will be selected for the final round on the basis of :
    • Quality and originality of the idea
    • Is it solving the problem or not?
    • The way presentation of ideas are made
    • Scope for implementation

Judging criteria
  • In the final round, each team will be allotted a time of at most 5 minutes for presenting their idea and hence participants are required to prepare the slides accordingly. Only one of the team members is supposed to present.
  • Presentation will be followed by 3 minutes of Q&A from the judges.
  • Presentation of ideas will be judged based on the following main criteria:

INNOVATION

How ‘innovative’ is the idea? Does it address the issues relevant to the problem statement?

Feasibility of Implementation

Is the idea feasible for execution? If so,how can it be executed within a specific amount of time?

Sustainability

Is the idea sustainable and cost-effective?If so,how capable and scalable is it to the changing trends of technology?

Impact

Does the idea possess a potential to create an impactful solution to the problem?If so, how does it raise awareness among the people in the society?
