Panel discussion 1

Topic
Impact of social media on human behaviour and society industry?

Panelists
Dr D.N Sujatha
Ashwini Appaji
Dr Kavitha sooda
Spoorthy U

Date - 28/08/2020
Timing- 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Social media enables us to stay in touch with friends and family when we are far apart, especially in times like these. Connecting with an old, forgotten friend on social media is amazing, scrolling through our feed, reliving old memories makes us nostalgic. All that was fine until social media became an endless rabbithole that feeds off our feeling of FOMO and want of validation. In recent years social media has impacted our mental and emotional health more than we know it. While social media has its benefits, it can never be a replacement for real-world connections. So how and where exactly is social media affecting human behaviour and society in general?

Topic
Development and sustainability of startups post covid-19

Panelists
Rajashekhar S
Udaykumar Birje
Parag thakre
Arun Narayan
Ananth Srinivasan
Amit Jain

Date - 29/08/2020

The past four to five months have changed many things- lifestyle, economy and the way we view things. Things look bleak for some companies while the others have skyrocketed. It seems like an ambiguous time for the startup ecosystem in India. We are hosting this discussion with our panelists so that the participants can gain an insight into the opportunities and risks related to developing and sustaining a startup in the post-covid times.
