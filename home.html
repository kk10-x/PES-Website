IEEE PES Student Congress - 2020
Welcome
BMSCE is honoured to host IEEE PES Student Congress. As is the case with most things these days, we are taking Student Congress to virtual heights. We bring to you an array of events from workshop to ideathon and everything in between. Over the course of three days- 28th, 29th and 30th of August we seek to provide a platform to exchange knowledge and technology and connect with student chapters spanning throughout the country.
